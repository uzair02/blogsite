Top Five Sports

There are a lot of sports played in the world today. Sports are played at two levels one is professionally and other is just for fun. Sport is considered one of the most healthy activity in the world, it is a kind of exercise because your whole body is forced to move.

Benefits Of Sports On Your Health:

  • Lower risks of heart diseases.
  • Improve strength.
  • Improves breathing.
  • Controls cholesterol.
  • Releases stress.
  • Improves mental health.
  • Better sleep.

Now let’s talk about the top five sports:

Cricket:

Cricket is considered a very popular sports among everyone of every age. It is an extremely popular sport with almost 2.5 billion fans globally. It is also considered one of the best sports in terms of profession. There are at least 33 teams playing cricket internationally in the world. Cricket is a very healthy sport also as it requires intense movement of body and it improves the speed of your reflexes. The pitch of the cricket is 22 yard long. There are many tournaments of cricket worldwide such as ODIs, test and T20. The biggest competition in world is world cup. If we talk about cricket at local level that is mostly played for fun hence there are not much rules and regulations involved.

Soccer(football):

Football is most popular in terms of popularity across the world with nearly 3.5 billions fans. Football is played between 2 teams with a spherical ball and it contains 2 keepers which prevents opponent team to goal. Football is also one of the most beneficial sports for health as it requires a lot of running. popular among kids and teenagers they like to watch it as well as to play it. Football requires practice to play and goal you need to develop quick reflexes to understand your opponent strategy as well as to do a goal bayern munchen the Germany team is at top place and Liverpool an England team is at the second place.

Basketball:

Football basically demands a high stamina if you want to play in professionally. Football is very Basketball sport is very popular in different parts of the world especially in America. Basketball is also played very vast as a profession basketball is played with a spherical ball and as the name suggest you have ton pull the ball in basket but this game is played with hands and it requires a lot of running. Basketball has almost 500 million fans around the world. Basketball didn’t gained much popularity as cricket and football but it has a huge fanbase.

Hockey:

Hockey is a pretty old game and it is played mostly at international level or local tournament. Hockey is played since 3rd century BC. In terms of popularity hockey has a great fan base for almost over 2 billion fans. According to the survey of NHL (National Hockey League) there are at least 37 teams playing hockey. Hockey is also one of the best physical activities as it requires movement of your hand which is used to control hockey stick and movement of feet for intense running.

Volleyball:

Volleyball originating from US is also considered a very good activities in term of exercise as it requires a lot of movement of body. Volleyball is one of the sports which are played internationally and at local level. Volleyball has a fanbase of almost 950 million people around the world. Volleyball is the 5th most popular sport in the world.
